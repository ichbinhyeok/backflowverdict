## Rules
- Utility or water-authority page is the canonical SEO unit.
- This product is local compliance plus next action, not generic plumbing SEO.
- Residential and commercial workflows must stay separate when rules differ.
- Official rules outrank all sponsor content.
- Official guidance and sponsor or directory content must never be blended.
- `approved-testers` exists only when an official authority list exists.
- `find-a-tester` is non-official directory routing only.
- City routes are alias handling, not the core page strategy.
- Texas is the current seeded baseline, not a permanent product boundary. Do not assume the project should stay Texas-only.
- Additional states are allowed when they introduce representative utility patterns and can meet the same source, freshness, and trust gates as Texas.
- Freshness and verification are launch-critical. Do not keep stale pages indexed just to preserve coverage.
- Keep JSON and CSV as the source of truth through launch. Do not introduce a database as a launch rewrite.
- Update `ops/context_tracker.md` after meaningful changes.
