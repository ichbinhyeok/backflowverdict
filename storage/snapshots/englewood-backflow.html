City of Englewood Backflow Prevention and Cross-Connection Control Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Englewood is a useful Colorado utility because the program is driven by change-of-use notifications, city re-surveys, and real hazard triggers.

Operational highlights

  • Englewood says its BPCCC program protects the city's water supply from pollutants and contamination entering the drinking water system.
  • The city requires notification when customers install new cross-connections or change water usage.
  • Examples include irrigation systems, boilers, water storage, and whole-facility treatment systems.
