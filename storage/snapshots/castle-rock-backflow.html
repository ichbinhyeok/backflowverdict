Castle Rock Water Cross-Connection and Backflow Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Castle Rock is a strong Colorado utility because annual testing, tester certification, and the official tester list all line up on the same utility workflow.

Operational highlights

  • Castle Rock says annual testing is required and compliance is managed by Castle Rock Water.
  • Only certified backflow assembly testers are permitted to test within the Town of Castle Rock.
  • The official tester PDF says testers must furnish current certification, gauge calibration, and insurance.
