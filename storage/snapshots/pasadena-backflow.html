Pasadena Water and Power Cross-Connection Control Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Pasadena is a strong Los Angeles-area utility because the utility publishes annual due-date testing, failed-test repair workflow, explicit high-hazard property examples, and directs customers to the Los Angeles County certified tester directory rather than a Pasadena-only approved list.

Operational highlights

  • Each backflow prevention assembly must be tested at least once a year by the due date on the PWP notice.
  • PWP directs customers to Los Angeles County certified backflow prevention testing businesses.
  • Failed assemblies must be repaired by a C36 plumbing contractor and the passing retest sent back to PWP.
