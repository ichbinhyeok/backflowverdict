City of Lafayette Backflow Compliance Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Lafayette is a strong Front Range utility because it publishes clear annual due dates by device type, a BSI workflow, and enforcement language with fees and shutoff risk.

Operational highlights

  • Irrigation systems are due by May 31 and other devices by August 31.
  • Lafayette runs the reporting workflow through BSI Online.
  • Late fees and water shutoff are part of the city enforcement path.
