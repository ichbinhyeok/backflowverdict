City of Anaheim Cross Connection Control source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL. Anaheim is a strong Southern California utility because the public program combines annual testing rules, approved-list gating, electronic submission, and utility standards for irrigation and fireline work.

Operational highlights

  • Anaheim says backflow devices must be tested annually and repaired and retested if defective.
  • Tests must be performed by an Orange County Health Care Agency certified tester with a valid Anaheim business license.
  • The city routes users through an approved tester page and electronic result workflow instead of mailed or emailed reports.
