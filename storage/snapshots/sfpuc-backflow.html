San Francisco Public Utilities Commission Backflow Prevention Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

San Francisco is commercially useful because SF.gov exposes a certified tester list with real companies instead of making users guess.

Operational highlights

  • San Francisco exposes a certified tester list through SF.gov rather than a generic contractor directory.
  • The certified tester list includes tester names, phone numbers, email addresses, and websites.
  • This makes SFPUC useful as both a compliance page and a high-intent approved-tester route.
