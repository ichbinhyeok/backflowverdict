Sarasota County Public Utilities Cross-Connection Control Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Sarasota County is a high-quality Florida county utility because it combines customer notices, hazard IDs, approved contractor lists, and separate fire-protection contractor routing.

Operational highlights

  • Sarasota County says all non-residential customers must maintain maximum protection at the water meter.
  • Customers who receive a notice and do not respond may face code enforcement action or shutoff timelines.
  • The county publishes separate approved contractor and fire protection contractor lists.
