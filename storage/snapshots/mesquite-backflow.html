Mesquite backflow source snapshot

Captured for the file-backed registry on 2026-04-04 by reviewer TL.

Durable excerpt: Mesquite requires installation testing and annual testing for non-residential assemblies, uses a city-registered tester list, charges a yearly per-assembly fee, and requires the Backflow Inspector to witness the test. The City also publishes separate irrigation and commercial assembly guides, including fire line requirements.
