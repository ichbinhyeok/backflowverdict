City of Aspen Cross Connection Control source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL. Aspen publishes a real backflow workflow: BSI notifications, annual testing, a city-hosted certified tester list, and explicit device guidance for irrigation and fire systems.

Operational highlights

  • Aspen says initial notifications come from BSI and testers must submit reports online via BSI.
  • Containment devices are tested at least annually, and the city sends anniversary reminders.
  • The city-hosted flyer lists certified testers for domestic and irrigation work as well as fire work.
