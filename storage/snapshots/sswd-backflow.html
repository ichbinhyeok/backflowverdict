Sacramento Suburban Water District Cross-Connection Control Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

SSWD is a strong district utility because it clearly defines covered service classes and runs a tester-entry portal.

Operational highlights

  • SSWD says all commercial, irrigation, institutional, industrial, and multifamily connections must install approved assemblies.
  • Certain single-family residential service connections also require a backflow prevention assembly.
  • SSWD-approved testers enter results through the district portal.
