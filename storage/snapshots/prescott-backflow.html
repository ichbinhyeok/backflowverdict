City of Prescott Backflow Prevention Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Prescott is a strong Northern Arizona utility because the city publishes installation-plus-annual testing language, approved testers, and utility engineering standards that keep irrigation and fire connections inside the same workflow.

Operational highlights

  • Prescott says required devices are tested at installation and annually thereafter.
  • The city publishes an approved backflow prevention assembly tester PDF.
  • Irrigation and fire protection connections sit inside the same utility containment workflow.
