Manatee County Utilities Cross Connection Control Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Manatee County is a high-intent Florida county utility because the program uses real letter deadlines, county-contractor intervention, and portal filing deadlines instead of generic education copy.

Operational highlights

  • Manatee County says annual testing must be completed within 30 days from the date of the county letter.
  • Final notices leave seven days before the county contract plumber is scheduled and at least $50 per assembly is charged.
  • Registered contractors must submit reports through the portal within 7 days, and failed tests must be entered within 1 business day.
