Town of Prescott Valley Backflow Prevention Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Prescott Valley is useful because the town publishes a real certified-tester handout, says listed companies are not specifically endorsed, and still ties required assemblies to installation and annual testing.

Operational highlights

  • Required assemblies are tested at installation and annually thereafter.
  • The tester handout says none of the listed companies are specifically endorsed by the Town.
  • Accepted testers must keep current certification, test-kit calibration, and town business-license records on file.

Official sources

  • Prescott Valley certified backflow testers handout
  • Prescott area engineering standards chapters 4 to 6
