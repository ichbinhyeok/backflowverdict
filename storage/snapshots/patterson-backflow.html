City of Patterson Backflow Prevention Device Annual Test Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL. Patterson is a strong California local page because the city explains the annual mailing cycle, requires owners to notify the city of the approved tester they chose, and publishes a public table of approved outside testers.

Operational highlights

  • Periodic test and maintenance letters are sent in January.
  • Owners respond by calling or emailing the city with their selected approved outside tester.
  • If an owner does not respond, Patterson says the city will perform the tests for that cycle.
