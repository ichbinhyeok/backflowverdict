JEA Backflow Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

JEA is one of the strongest Florida utilities because it openly separates commercial annual testing, residential irrigation rules, and a live qualified-tester list with clear utility rules.

Operational highlights

  • JEA says all commercial water services must have RP assemblies installed, maintained, and tested annually.
  • Commercial failed tests must be repaired or replaced and retested within 30 days.
  • Residential irrigation backflow devices are tested every two years, and JEA may dispatch a tester if results are not received within 45 days.
