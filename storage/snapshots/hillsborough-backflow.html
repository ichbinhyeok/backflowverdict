Hillsborough County Water Resources backflow source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL. Hillsborough County publishes annual testing language, a registered-tester gate, 48-hour failed-test notice, and permit-linked reporting for installation and replacement work.

Operational highlights

  • All property owners with a backflow assembly must have it tested annually.
  • Only a registered Hillsborough County certified tester can test the assembly.
  • The county requires notification within 48 hours of any failed backflow preventer assembly.
