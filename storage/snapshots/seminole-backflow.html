Seminole County Cross Connection Control source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL. Seminole County is a strong Florida county utility because it publishes an official tester list, annual testing language, a 20-day repair clock, and a separate residential irrigation meter workflow.

Operational highlights

  • Assemblies are tested annually by county-approved testers.
  • Failed devices must be repaired and retested within 20 days.
  • Residential irrigation meter installations also require testing by a Seminole County approved tester.
