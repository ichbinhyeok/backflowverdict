City of Arvada Backflow and Cross-Connection Control Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Arvada is one of the strongest Colorado city utilities because it combines a July 31 deadline, non-compliance fees, and a public certified tester PDF.

Operational highlights

  • Arvada says all assemblies must be inspected and tested annually.
  • The city moved all assemblies to a July 31 annual test deadline.
  • A fee schedule for non-compliant customers was added beginning in 2023.
