City of Flagstaff Backflow Prevention Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Flagstaff is a strong Arizona utility because it mixes annual testing, BSI result handling, meter-adjacent installation rules, and a residential fire-sprinkler waiver in one city-owned workflow.

Operational highlights

  • Flagstaff says residents and businesses with contamination risk are required to install backflow assemblies.
  • The city requires annual tests for assemblies in the program and sends courtesy notices when testing is due.
  • Repairs require immediate retest, and fire sprinkler owners can apply for a residential waiver in limited cases.
