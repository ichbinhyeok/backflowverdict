Talty SUD backflow source snapshot

Captured for the file-backed registry on 2026-04-04 by reviewer TL.

Durable excerpt: Talty SUD requires installation testing for irrigation assemblies, annual testing for health-hazard and commercial properties, May 1 or November 1 reporting deadlines, RPZ replacement for OSSF properties when a failing DCV cannot be repaired, and district registration for testers. The district also publishes the tester list, report form, and tester registration form.
