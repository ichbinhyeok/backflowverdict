Santa Rosa Backflow Prevention Program source snapshot

Verified 2026-04-05 by TL.

Santa Rosa is a high-quality California utility page because annual testing, 30-day reminder timing, tester authorization, and enforcement all sit on public city documents.

Operational highlights

  • Santa Rosa says backflow devices are tested every year by authorized testers.
  • The city sends reminder postcards about 30 days before the due date and expects the report within 30 days of that due date.
  • Late reports can trigger an annual notice fee and a 30-day shutoff notice.
